A WCAG-compliant browser extension that fetches and analyses privacy policies against India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act. Built with Manifest V3, Jina AI Reader API, and Cloudflare Worker proxy. Five popup UI states, accessible design, URL-based policy fetching.
